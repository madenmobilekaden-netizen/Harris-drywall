Our Story

Building Trust, One
Wall at a Time.

Cale Harris grew up in the drywall trade — the third generation of his family to master the craft. He brought that heritage and his experience to Osage Beach, Camdenton, and Lake Ozark, building Harris Group Drywall Solutions on a single promise: show up on time, communicate clearly, and never leave a job site anything less than spotless.

That commitment has earned five-star Google reviews from homebuilders, property managers, and commercial contractors alike — from a 60-room Holiday Inn Express renovation to custom new builds and everything in between.
